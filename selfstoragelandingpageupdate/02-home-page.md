### Section 2: The problem

**Headline:** Every empty unit is a renter who found someone else first.

**Body:** Most self-storage searches start on a phone and end within a three to five mile radius. If your facility is buried on page two of Google Maps, if your Google Business Profile is half-filled, or if your website takes eight seconds to load, renters keep scrolling. The REITs invest heavily in local visibility. Independent operators often do not. That gap is where your units stay empty.
