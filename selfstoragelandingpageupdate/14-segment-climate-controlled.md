#### Section 2: What climate-controlled facilities actually need

Renters who want climate-controlled storage search differently than general storage renters. They are storing furniture, musical instruments, documents, electronics, or art. They are willing to pay a premium. They are comparing facilities on specifics: is it temperature-controlled only, or humidity-controlled too? What temperature range is maintained? Does the facility have 24-hour climate monitoring?

If your pages do not answer those questions, a premium renter moves on.

#### Section 3: How we work with climate-controlled facilities

Three focus areas:

1. **Climate-specific keyword coverage.** We target climate-controlled, temperature-controlled, humidity-controlled, and the long-tail variants renters actually use.
2. **Content that converts premium renters.** Guides on what to store in climate control, temperature ranges to expect, and how climate control differs from basic indoor storage.
3. **Local SEO that highlights the premium offering.** Google Business Profile attributes, photos, and posts that make climate control visible in search results.

#### Section 4: Typical results

Facilities with strong climate-controlled offerings typically see:

- Top three rankings for climate-controlled keywords within 90 to 150 days
- Higher average reservation value, since climate-controlled units convert at premium rates
- Lower churn, because renters storing high-value items tend to stay longer
