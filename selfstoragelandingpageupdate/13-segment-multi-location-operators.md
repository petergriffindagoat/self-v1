#### Section 2: What multi-location operators actually need

Running five, fifteen, or fifty facilities creates marketing challenges that single-location operators never face. Your facilities can cannibalize each other in overlap zones. Your review velocity varies wildly across locations. Your on-site staff follows different processes at different sites. And your leadership team needs one view of what is working portfolio-wide.

You need a partner with systems, not just tactics.

#### Section 3: How we work with multi-location operators

Four focus areas:

1. **Portfolio-wide local SEO architecture.** One system for location pages, one workflow for Google Business Profile management, one review program, applied consistently across every facility.
2. **Overlap zone resolution.** We map where your facilities compete with each other and we choose which facility wins which keyword, so internal cannibalization stops.
3. **Operational alignment.** We train your on-site teams on the review program, the photo upload cadence, and any on-site tasks that support local SEO.
4. **Roll-up reporting for leadership.** Portfolio-level dashboards for ownership and operations leadership. Facility-level dashboards for site managers.

#### Section 4: Typical results

For operators at five-plus facilities, the six-month outcome is usually:

- Every facility ranking in the top three for its primary keyword
- Portfolio-wide direction request volume up 2x to 3x
- Review velocity standardized at 8+ new reviews per facility per month
